About

Software System Development is a graduate-level course that equips students with a comprehensive understanding of the principles, tools, and practices involved in designing and building modern software systems, spanning OS, Networks, SE, and Databases (relational and NoSQL).

Learners gain hands-on experience with Bash, SQL, NoSQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, front-end frameworks, REST, Python, Flask, and deployment workflows, emphasizing scalable, maintainable, and secure systems.

Designed for students with fundamental programming skills, the course bridges theory and practice for complex research and industry projects.
